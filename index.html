Wasteland Survival Calculator

One clean embeddable Fallout calculator, styled like a cracked old terminal and powered by deterministic math. Build your S.P.E.C.I.A.L. loadout, lock in two perks, choose a companion, survive eight scenario questions, and see how long you realistically last on the surface.

Demo page auto-boots the widget through data-wasteland-calculator. The real embed API is window.WastelandCalculator.init(container, config).
